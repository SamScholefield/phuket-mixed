Rules
Pool Stage
  • 2 Pools of 5 teams — round robin format (4–5 matches per team)
  • 10-minute matches, no halftime
  • Team listed first taps off and runs away from the club house
  • Must have 1 inclusivity player on pitch at all times
  • Scoring: 3 match points for the first try · 2 match points for every subsequent try
  • 3 table points for a win · 1 table point for a draw · 0 table points for a loss
  • Tiebreaker: (1) Head-to-head · (2) Highest aggregate scored · (3) Best points difference · (4) Coin toss
Finals Stage
  • Knockout format — 12-minute matches (2 × 6 min halves, 2 min halftime)
  • 1st & 2nd place in each pool → Cup semi-finals
  • 3rd & 4th place in each pool → Plate semi-finals
  • 5th place teams → Bowl Final direct (no semi)
  • Scoring: 3 match points for first try · 2 match points for every subsequent try
  • Must have 1 inclusivity player on pitch at all times
Drop-Off (Nil–Nil at Full Time)
  • Team that did not tap off at kick-off taps off for the drop-off
  • Reduce to 4 players per team (minimum 1 inclusivity player)
  • Play for 2 minutes — first try wins
  • Exception: if tap-off team scores on their first drive, other team may equalise — then first try wins
  • If no score after 2 minutes: remove one further player, continue (retain 1 inclusivity player)
  • Play resumes at same touch count & position as when stopped
  • Player interchanges allowed provided inclusivity requirements are met
